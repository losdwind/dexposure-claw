\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We characterised the operating point of a deployment-ready
LLM-agent pipeline that routes the LLM through structured
forecasted evidence for DeFi exposure-network supervision. The
takeaway for practitioners is operational: routing the LLM
through a typed evidence bundle raises ticket F1 by $207\%$ and
explanation quality by $9.4\%$ under a tier-above judge (with a
cross-family panel preserving the ordering), at a measurable
false-intervention cost partially absorbed by safety gates.
\benchname{} makes that trade-off visible; \sysname{}'s
audit-log release makes it retunable without re-running the
pipeline.
