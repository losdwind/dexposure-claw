\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:discussion-conclusion}
\label{sec:lessons}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We presented \sysname{}, a forecast-grounded agentic supervision
system, and \benchname{}, a reproducible harness with reference
implementations and ticket-level audit logs for DeFi risk monitoring.
Across five years of weekly exposure graphs, structured forecast
evidence improves supervisory coverage, but safe operation depends on
decision-model calibration rather than the forecaster alone. The
industry lesson is an auditable path toward human-in-the-loop DeFi
supervision: forecast-grounded evidence, explicit FIR, gate states,
and conservative human control before any live deployment.
